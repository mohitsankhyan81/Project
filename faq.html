Frequently Asked Questions

What is plastic waste?

Plastic waste refers to any discarded plastic item that is not reused or recycled. It often ends up in landfills or the ocean, causing long-term environmental damage.

Why is plastic harmful to the environment?

Plastic doesn't break down easily. It can pollute soil and water, and is dangerous to animals who might eat or get entangled in it.

How can I reduce my plastic use?

Use reusable bags, bottles, and containers. Choose products with minimal packaging and support recycling initiatives.
